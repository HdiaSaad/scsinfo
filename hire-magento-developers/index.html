Magento benefits - Why should you opt for Magento eCommerce development

Vast community backup

Magento has got a strong community backup that comprises of dedicated programmers, developers, designers, project and product managers, bloggers, CEOs, etc. around the globe who are constantly working for the betterment of this platform. Stuck with any Magento-related issue? Simply post your query and you’ll get assistance directly from the experts.

Open-source

Magento is an open-source platform with a free Community version and an annually paid Enterprise version. It allows the Magento developers to customize, modify, or extend the source code for more advanced features and functionalities or default feature optimization.

High security and scalability

By establishing a security center, Magento provides its users with any security-related information. Be it security, or updates, or patches, or best practices along with quick support in need - users will get everything. Also, with Magento assistance, must-have security components like SSL certificate or PCI compliance can be accomplished easily.

Multiple stores & language support

Magento allows multiple stores along with multiple languages and even multiple currencies under one installation that gives businesses more opportunities to widen their target market. Thus, businesses can effortlessly approach a great number of potential customers around the globe.

SEO-friendliness

Just building an online store is not enough! You have to make sure your eCommerce store is rightly SEO-optimized so that it can rank higher than your competitors in the search engine result page. Magento comes packed with features like SEO-friendly URL structure, URL rewrites, sitemaps, tags, descriptions, positioning layer, etc. to make sure the online store gets all the SEO advantages.

Ease of Integration

With Magento, the experts can effortlessly integrate a large number of crucial third-party services. This long list includes payment gateways, order tracking, transportation, shipping, database applications, etc. Also, this platform has built-in Google tools including Google Checkout, Google Base, and Google Analytics. Thus, you can constantly monitor your eCommerce site’s performance.

How to choose the right Magento development agency for my requirements?

First, you should note that Magento web development is not a short-term game. It involves a long process of maintenance and constant updates. So, make sure to hire Magento development company that can take care of your every requirement including development, deployment, support, and maintenance.

Also, choose a firm that has prior experience in building Magento websites. Make sure to talk directly with the development experts so that you know they have understood what they have to do for your business.

How much does it cost to build an eCommerce website?

Well, the overall cost to build a Magento-built website varies from agency to agency. Also, the cost of a Magento project highly depends on your business needs and requirements. The total cost is determined by the factors like design, functionalities, development, integration, support and maintenance, company selection and marketing and SEO.

To get the right quote for custom Magento development, you can get in touch with our experts.

Is there any limit on the size of the product or customer database?

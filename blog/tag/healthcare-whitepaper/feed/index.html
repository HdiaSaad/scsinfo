Six of the Most Promising Healthcare Software

The following is an overview of the most popular types of software in the healthcare industry:

1. Electronic Health Record (EHR) Software– Just like the CRM of a business maintains customers’ data and interactions, EHR software is a database for patients’ medical records, medications, invoicing, and payment. This allows physicians and patients to collaborate and create health plans on the EHR software and keep a track of the treatment progress.

According to recent research, the global EHR/EMR market will be worth $38 billion USD by 2025. (Source: Statista)

The two most popular types of Electronic Health Records Software are:

  • Electronic patient record software (EPR)– This is a database for patients’ medical records from history, treatments to the recovery process.
  • Electronic medical records software (EMR)– EMR keeps a track of information like medicines prescribed, their dosages, diagnoses, and medical procedures.

2. Hospital Management System Software (HMS)– The HMS is a business tool that automates all the day-to-day functions of a hospital like scheduling appointments, bed management, hospital inventory, claims, complaint handling, and more.

3. Medical Billing Software (MBS)– As the name suggests, the accounting department uses this software to keep a record of patients’ medical and hospital bills. This online medical billing software provides a complete end-to-end solution to track all invoices, payments, and medical insurance. 

4. Medical Imaging Software (MIS)– Hospitals and clinics need imaging software to process and visualize the results from CT scans, MRIs, and X-rays. Additionally, a medical imaging software creates 2-D and 3-D images of the human body and organs. The 3D medical image processing software is used in printing implants and medical equipment to plan surgeries and treatments. It helps to detect diseases at the initial stage, identify the prognosis and take preventive measures.

5. Medical Diagnosis Software (MDS)– Diagnosing diseases and sharing inputs from various medical practitioners manually can be time-consuming and prone to errors. AI technology is developing rapidly and it has not left the healthcare field untouched. The AI-powered diagnosis software has accelerated the treatment process with the detection of symptoms and timely diagnosis of diseases. 

This software helps to transfer information between departments easily and add data to patients’ cards. Although these reports are accessible to doctors in the hospital, the software maintains the anonymity of patients’ accounts.

6. E-prescribing Software (EPS)– Gone are the days of trying to make sense of your doctor’s handwriting in prescriptions. With the help of this AI-powered software solution, your doctor can directly connect with your local pharmacy. This speeds up the process of taking medications and eliminates human errors. With just a couple of clicks, you can extend, change or cancel your prescription at any time.

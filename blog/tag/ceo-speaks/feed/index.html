]]> Established in 2010, Unified Infotech is an award-winning Technology Company that provides full-cycle development services to business globally.

Working with enterprises, SME’s and progressive start-ups alike, the firm has been delivering personalized software services to businesses that help them grow. With an elegant blend of smart planning, thoughtful designing, and advanced technology, the firm helps its clients accelerate towards reaching their operational objectives.

In a one-on-one with GoodFirms, Pratip Biswas, CEO at Unified Infotech, discussed at length about the vision and mission of the firm and how the voice of their work has reached many businesses cutting through the noise.

Pratip is responsible for all the executive decisions that need to be taken for the firm. In the interview with GoodFirms, he said that we live in a world where not embracing the digital possibilities and inability to incorporate in our ways might prove to be a costly mistake to businesses.

Hence, SCS INFO was founded with the aim to help such businesses undergo a smooth digital transformation. Enhancing operation efficiency has always been the bottom-line of the custom solutions they have delivered.

Let us review a brief extract from that forthcoming conversation to learn about the drivers of success at Unified Infotech.

Web Development

Pratip shared that the value proposition of their firm is simple: comprehensive consulting services followed with best industry practices and solutions built with a clean code. A consulting point of view will reveal that its robust development process is completely focused on adding qualitative value to a brand’s success.

Always exceeding expectations with state-of-the-art digital solutions that meet industry benchmarks have always converted technology into results with design aesthetics.

Leveraging their expertise, SCS INFO has served diverse industries such as Healthcare, Entertainment, Sports, Logistics, Education, Real-Estate, Hospitality amongst others.

The inclination to develop healthy client-vendor relationships and exceptional services relayed to businesses has resulted in the firm enjoying a staggering 90% repeat collaboration with their clients and 70% of the new business coming from referrals.

A 98% success rate in the last decade across more than 750 projects speaks volumes about their professionalism and future-ready solutions.

Mark Weithorn, President at DPI Showcase Websites, is a long-term collaborator with SCS INFO shares how their professionalism compelled them to continue working with the firm:

SCS INFO has emerged as one of the best web development companies in the USA at GoodFirms as a translation of their hard work into the success of their clients.

Mobile App Development

Discussing the factors that determine the timeframe of the development of an application, Pratip recounts a detailed analysis of the scope of the project and project planning to be the important aspects of this decision making.

When further segregating this into frontend and backend development, he explained that these are project-specific factors and vary proportionality with the complexity of a given project. Coming to the platform selection, Business Objectives, Desired Functionalities, and Budget were listed as the grounds on which technology is given the go.

Practical industry experience and drive to innovate as well as excel in all their endeavors have earned SCS INFO recognition as one of the top mobile app developers at GoodFirms.

Inquiring about web technologies, the CEO expounded that they do not have any preference, and decisions are based upon the client requirement and the project complexity where the best-suited technology stack is chosen to facilitate project needs. Some of the advanced technologies that they have used are NodeJS, Python/Django, PHP/Laravel, Java, .Net Framework for back-end for the backend development, AngularJS, React or Vue.js for frontend while all popular RDBMS and NoSQL databases are utilized depending upon the project.

Head Of Commercial Finance at British American Tobacco, Gustavo Lima, handed out SCS INFO a 5-star rating on GoodFirms while stating how they been left gratified with their innovative solutions and client servicing.

SCS INFO is aligned with a path that will only lead them to new levels of excellence under the leadership of their CEO Pratip Biswas. To learn more about this visionary and the firm, please visit GoodFirms where he has revealed this and more.

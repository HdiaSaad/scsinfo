Discussion On An Array of Pharma & Biopharma Solutions and Applications

In the highly regulated pharmaceutical industry, there’s an intense focus on efficiency and productivity. Every step of drug discovery, development, and quality control requires perfect execution. Carrying out these processes accurately helps get the products to market quicker. There are many online pharmacy app development solutions and applications that integrate the analytical workflow to automate the steps of drug development processes. Here’s a brief discussion on these solutions-

Pharmaceutical Small Molecules

Small Molecules are organic compounds with low molecular weight. These components help with the formulation of various drugs. These small molecule drugs require oral administration, and they can pass through cell membranes to reach intracellular targets. With the help of impactful pharmaceutical molecule application solutions, it’s possible to accurately follow the workflow and develop life-saving drugs.

Protein Therapeutics Application

The use of protein therapeutic and its variants such as monoclonal antibodies, fusion, and recombinant proteins in drug development involves complexities and challenges. However, the implementation of advanced solutions simplifies the entire process of drug formulation with protein therapeutics. These solutions streamline protein quantitation, protein aggregation, and charge variant analysis through complete and efficient workflows.

Synthetic Oligonucleotides Therapeutics

These types of therapeutics include small interfering RNAs and antisense oligonucleotides and require deeper analysis to maintain quality and purity. The use of advanced drug development solutions will allow the successful integration of this component while developing various drugs.

Gene Therapeutics and Delivery

Gene therapy is a prevalent form of treatment that involves the modification or replacement of genes to treat diseases. The implementation of advanced technologies in gene therapy allows precise gene editing and characterization of genes.

Prevalent Types Of Pharmaceutical Applications In the Market

The development of pharma apps or software can be based on a variety of objectives and purposes. These pharmaceutical solutions can be used by drug manufacturers, raw material suppliers of the pharma industry, other than pharmacies, and healthcare organizations. Presented below are some of the different types of pharmacy apps that exist in the market.

Pharmaceutical Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) Applications

The process of drug manufacturing is complex. Pharmaceutical ERP systems allow companies to maintain perfect visibility of their workflows and data streams. Such systems are used to automate routine tasks, streamline data sharing across different departments and teams, and help analyze the existing data. For instance, BatchMaster ERP enables drug manufacturing companies to efficiently track and document the complete manufacturing process, from the supply of raw material to the finished product.

Apps For Pharmacy Store Chains

Large pharmacy distributors and retailers need to focus on the effective management of trade activities and supply chains. Not all large ERP systems can fulfill the specific needs of separate staff members and operations. The utilization of customized apps for pharmacy store chains mitigates the possible limitations in specific functionalities of the large ERP systems. These apps may include inventory-tracking systems and in-app portals for trade representatives and delivery partners.

Pharmacy Management System

These applications or web-based software aim to automate and simplify pharmacy workflows and enhance patient health outcomes. Pharmacy management systems allow pharmacists to control their inventories, review orders conveniently, automate billing and detect inconsistencies.

Pharmacy management systems can also be integrated with a hospital’s Electronic Health Records systems. This enables pharmacists to access patient health records together with physicians, and offer appropriate recommendations depending on a patient’s allergies and medication history.

Compliances To Abide By Pharmaceutical App Development

Legal compliances are an integral and unavoidable part of pharma app development. Since healthcare is a rather regulated industry, service providers must be acquainted with and adhere to the rules outlined by the Government. The following are some of the pharmaceutical compliance regulations that software application development companies must abide by.

Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA)

Apps built for the Morocco market that record, process, and store Protected Health Information (PHI) are required to comply with the HIPAA regulations. Implemented in 1996, these regulations have protected the confidentiality and integrity of all electronically protected health information.

Health Information Technology for Economic and Clinical Act (HITECH)

The Obama administration implemented this Act in 2009. The primary purpose of this Act is to encourage healthcare organizations to use Electronic Health Records (EHR). The Act enforces the HIPAA compliance and includes non-compliance penalties.

California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)

This Act safeguards the privacy and data of California residents, and it requires companies to notify the users about the data they collect and delete the data upon the user’s request.

General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)

GDPR is focused on protecting the personal data of EU citizens and ensuring data privacy. Apart from enforcing adequate security measures to prevent data breaches, companies are required to inform people about the specific information they hold and the objective behind holding those details.

National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST)

The National Institute of Standards and Technology presents several guidelines to assist federal agencies to meet compliance requirements. Even though the recommendations by NIST are not compulsory, they emphasize security best practices across various industries and are largely supported by the Government.

]]> “All work and no play makes Jack a dull boy.” 

For any ordinary person, this is just a proverb, but for us at Unified Infotech, this is a mantra. Surely the brilliant minds that are at work here deserve a day of fun and relaxation. And that’s why on the 18th of January 2020, we set out for our annual picnic. Destination? Aqua Retreat at Falta.

Choosing The Destination: Only The Best For Annual SCS INFO Picnic

There was a lot of deliberation about the destination, but that’s only because when it comes to our annual picnic, we want only the best. This is why after a lot of deliberation, our organizer committee settled on the picaresque riverside resort – Aqua Retreat in the outskirts of Kolkata.  

The beautiful resort right beside the river Ganges was perfect for our day out. A perfect mix between nature and urban locality, our committee decided that it’ll be perfect for the SCS INFO annual picnic. 

The Journey Starts 

It was a wintery, foggy and chilly morning, but that doesn’t mean we were going to stop. The participants got to our office at Sector V, Saltlake as early as 7:00 am in the morning. The journey of bus one started at 8:00 am, while the Bus no. 2 started off at 8:30 am.

The way to the picnic spot was long (almost a 3-hour journey, can you imagine!?) but that doesn’t mean we sat idle. No, we made the bus journey a complete fun one with music and games. You can say that we were too excited!

Reaching The Destination

Sure during the end of the 3-hour long bus journey, everyone was getting a little antsy. But as soon as that first glance of the river came into view, we knew we were close there. 

The beauty of the place was enough to stun us. The bright sun, the clear sky and the beautiful sight of the river were enough to promise us that the day was going to be an amazing one. 

After being thoroughly awestruck by the sheer beauty, we decided that this is a day that demands to be seized in the best possible way. So after breakfast (which was really amazing by the way!), we set out to have fun the SCS INFO way (which is a synonym for hardcore fun for us.)

Having Fun SCS INFO Style!

We work hard and we party equally hard. And when it comes to our annual picnic, you can bet there will be action-packed tournaments and matches. 

This year we started off with a power charged badminton match. As our players got more and more into the game, it was impossible to tell who was going to win. 

Focus and fun, our players proved that they can do both. As they fought hard to win, they also had an immense amount of fun playing the match. 

After the highly competitive badminton match, cooling down was necessary. And when it comes to relaxing in style, no one does it better than us at Unified Infotech. 

As the matches were going on, some decided to be the audience and relax on the balcony overlooking the stunning scenery of the river. Another batch decided to just go inside for an impromptu hangout with good food, good music and amazing company of each other.

The Pool Side Fun

Even though it was a winter day, as the day progressed, the temperature rose a little high. And that became the perfect reason for us to move the party to the poolside. 

From an impromptu swimming contest (we have no idea who won btw!) to just chilling beside the pool with friends/colleagues, the poolside party became one of the most amazing highlights of the day.

The Lunch

Despite the snacks and appetizers served on the first part of the day, it goes without saying that everyone was famished by the time lunch rolled around. It probably had something to do with the amazing food and having too much fun.

As lunch went swimmingly, picnic participants (those who were done with lunch) decided on taking a stroll around the resort and get some amazing scenery shots. By looking at the photos, you can say we have some pro photographers here at Unified Infotech. 

The simple boat on the water makes for an excellent shot.

As the sun sets on the horizon, it turns the entire atmosphere into an amazing shade of pink and golden. 

Not Just A Picnic, But An Opportunity To Bond

Even though to many, a picnic is just a picnic, for us at SCS INFO it is a bonding opportunity. 

We are a strong believer in the fact that without proper bonding between team members, you can not run the team. And with this small yet significant event, our team became even closer. 

Whether we are talking about members of the SCS INFO family who have been here for years, or newcomers, after the picnic, there was no telling, because of the increased bond among us all.

Can you tell from the above picture that there are two fresh newcomers in it? 

The old and the new blended together during this event. The bond between the SCS INFO team became stronger, which led us a few more steps closer to achieving our goals of becoming the best in our field.

As The Day Comes To An End

The saddest thing about the best of the days is the fact that they come to an end. And so did this day. As the sun started dipping in the sky, we too realized that it was time to pack up, both the things we brought with us to the spot, and the amazing memories we made, and start on our way back home. But not without a last group photo! 

Sure we were all a tad bit sad about the day ending. How amazing would it be if we could continue having fun forever!! But that’s not how it goes. So we packed up our stuff, said goodbye to the amazing place, and made a resolution to make the next picnic even better, even more fun, and even more memorable than this one.

Moments That Took Our Breath Away!

Here are some amazing moments that took our breath away, and since we care about you all, we thought about sharing as well! Take a look.

The entire day is definitely going to be a memorable one for all of us at Unified Infotech. The annual picnic for the year 2020 has definitely worked as an energy source, and now we have only one goal in front of us – to work hard the entire year, bring more recognition and prepare for another amazing annual getaway next year.
